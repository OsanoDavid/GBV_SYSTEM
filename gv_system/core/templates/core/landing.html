Recognizing Online Violence

Technology-facilitated gender-based violence manifests in various forms. Select a category during reporting to help the triage team react swiftly.

Cyberstalking

Persistent unwanted messaging, surveillance, location tracking, or relentless online observation loops.

Doxing & Intimate Leaks

Maliciously distributing personal data records, phone directories, or unconsented private imagery across public spaces.

Impersonation

Fabricating duplicate profiles or identity matrices to spread disinformation, commit fraud, or target reputations.

Threats & Hate Speech

Direct intimidation of physical harm, targeted hate speech campaigns, or systematic harassment patterns.
