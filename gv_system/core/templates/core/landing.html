Recognizing Online Violence

Technology-facilitated gender-based violence manifests in various forms. Select a category during reporting to help the triage team react swiftly.

💬

Cyberstalking

Persistent unwanted messaging, surveillance, location hacking, or relentless online monitoring.

🔒

Doxing & Image Sharing

Leaking private, unconsented intimate photographs or distributing personal contact strings maliciously.

👤

Impersonation

Creating malicious duplicate profiles or identities designed to defame, blackmail, or target victims.

⚠️

Threats & Hate Speech

Direct threats of bodily harm, sexual violence, or structured targeted hate campaigns across platforms.
