Recognizing Online Violence

Technology-facilitated gender-based violence manifests in various forms. Click a category to expand details.

Cyberstalking involves persistent, unwanted online observation or messaging aimed at intimidating or controlling a victim.
Doxing is the act of publishing private information about an individual (like address or phone number) without consent.
Impersonation occurs when someone creates a fraudulent profile using your name or photos to harm your reputation or commit fraud.
Hate speech includes targeted insults or threats made against individuals based on their identity or gender online.
